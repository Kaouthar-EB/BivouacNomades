foum zguid the gate to the sahara

Foum Zguid is the nearest town to the sahara of lake iriki and chegaga


Foum zguid is a small town in the province of tata, in the southeast of Morocco region of Souss Massa. it consist of 9,630 of people. the original tribe of Foum Zguid are from the tribe called oum hensh and the rest of the population are nomades coming from the sahara. since it is the nearest city to the sahara most of nomads built a house and live in this city due to many causes. the meaning of the two words foum and zguid according to the telling history is the mouth of the river. from foum zguid you can book your desert tour and daily excursion to the sahara of lake iriki and chegaga dunes. you need only one hour and a half to reach the sahara of lake iriki where you can have so many desert activities. the camel trekking is the best activity to do in the sahara of lake iriki due to the beautiful and different lanscape in the area. it is also less touristic and very calm and wide sahara. lake iriki was the meeting place for caravanes in the past. Nomades from Malli,Tchad, Algery, Bourkinafassou ad niger used to come to a place called still nowdays souaikka, which means the small market where they exchange products at that time. the touarig people bring slaves to help them hold the roap of the camels through the whole sahara to reach lake irikiwhile they are in the top of the camel. as soon as they arrive they exchange them with lake iriki local products such us henne, salt, milk ,,, or other products that the other caravanes bring. this is what makes the camel trekking more intersting in the sahara of lake iriki, you can experience the desert trekking exactly as the caravanes in the past. as well as the qouads biking , sand bording ...
