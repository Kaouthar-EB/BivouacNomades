What to do in Foum Zguid


Foum zguid is one of the main perfect starting point to organise your desert tour to the real Sahara of lake iriki and chegaga.
Either with a 4×4 , camel trekking or even quad biking. If you have a small car it is not possible to pass with it through the pist because it is hard roads but don't worry there is a safe park where you can leave your car in foum zguid, untill you come back from your Sahara experience.

If you have a small car it is not possible to pass with it through the post because it is hard roads but the there is safe park where you can leave your car in foum zguid untill you come back from your Sahara experience. All this activitieas are available in foum zguid. So many tourist do not know foum zguid Sahara because there is not much publicity about the city and the area. If you are coming from tafraout or Tata it is the perfect place for you to make a Sahara tour firstly because it is before zagora you will need only 2 hours to reach the Sahara of lake iriki and 2:30 to reach chegaga desert. While if you want to go to zagora to organise your desert tour you need 1:30 to 2 hours drive to reach zagora city and then from there you will need to return and make the sircle back to the desert and you will need more than 4 hours. If you come from Marrakech also it will be the close than Zagora or Merzouga and also it is the Sahara where you can have the full Authentic Sahara experience.

If you are short in time you can have one night in the desert, you will enjoy your camel ride to see the sun set from the top of the big dunes of Erg chegaga or lake iriki. This is a very beautiful feeling that you can find only in the Sahara. You will enjoy your stay in a desert camp in the middle of the dunes and the beautiful sky full of thousands stars and the beautiful milkyway.

Here you can choose if you want traditional standard camp or if you would like to be in a luxury desert camp. And the next morning you will take the 4×4 to return back to foum zguid in the way there also few things you can discover and experience. You can visit the nomadic school of lake iriki and see the history behind the non governmental association that help the nomade to learn how to write and read and also a little bit of calculation or you can enjoy a cup of tea with the local nomads of the area and see how they survive in the Sahara. You will also discover the history behind the national park of lake iriki and how it was as well as visiting the fossils. If you have also enough time and you would like to have a full Sahara experiences you can book a camel trekking to go deep in the Sahara and discover life of nomads and the beauty of the desert.

It is better to choose a local agency to organise your desert tour if you want to have a good desert experience. And because also it is very safe with them since they know the desert so well since some times it can be hard if you meet a strong sandstorm or when it is dark in the desert you need local nomad who know the Sahara and how to survive in the Sahara and because also he can show you better places and tell you about the history the stories and life in the desert. feel free to contact us if you need any help or if you have any questions about the sahara or if you would like us to organize your authentic desert tour.
