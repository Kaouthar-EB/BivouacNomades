lake iriki restaurant and hotel

Lake Iriki desert restaurant by bivouac les nomades is in the middle of the dunes of lake iriki behind the big dune of Erg Elmhzil. It is only 2 kilometer in the south side of the main pist in the middle of lake iriki. It is the best place either you are coming from foum zguid or from Mahamid elghizlane to have a little stop to relax in our restaurant and enjoy a fresh drink, coffe, mint tea, have a snack, lunch while enjoying the beautiful view of the dunes around the camp. You can also pass the night in our beautiful desert camp in the middle of the dunes of lake iriki. we accept individual, smal groups or even rallyes members. You just need to inform our team before your arrival.
